About

I am a researcher in biology with broad interests in ecological and evolutionary genomics. My research focuses on two main topics: structural variation (SV) and gene tree discordance (GTD). I combine experimental and computational approaches to investigate genome landscapes/architectures of SV and GTD in corals and primates, and to understand how those architectures drive the evolution of corals and primates.
